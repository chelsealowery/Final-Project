How Did We Get Started

Our journey began in a small garage, where two passionate sibling automotive enthusiasts, Justin and Chelsea Lowery, shared a common dream – to create a haven for car lovers where quality craftsmanship and exceptional service reign supreme.

With years of experience in the automotive industry and a shared vision for excellence, they decided to combine their skills and expertise to establish what would soon become known as "Precision Auto Works". Fuelled by their love for cars and driven by their commitment to perfection, Justin and Chelsea embarked on a mission to redefine the standards of automotive care in their community.

Despite facing numerous challenges along the way, their unwavering dedication and relentless pursuit of excellence propelled them forward. Through hard work, determination, and a steadfast commitment to their customers, Precision Auto Works quickly gained a reputation for delivering top-notch services and exceeding expectations.

Today, their small garage has evolved into a thriving business, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of skilled technicians who share John and Sarah's passion for cars. While the journey may have had its twists and turns, one thing remains constant – their dedication to providing unparalleled automotive services and creating lasting relationships with their valued customers.
